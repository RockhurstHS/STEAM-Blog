On Thursday, April 26 the Communication Arts Department hosted the 5th annual Digital Media Festival- The Roscars.  Projects from students enrolled in Introduction to Moviemaking and Editing, Advanced Film making, Digital Photography and Broadcasting 1&2 were showcased.  Awards were given to students from each class.
